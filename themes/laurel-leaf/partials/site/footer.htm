The Story about the Laurel Leaf

The original Laurel Leaf pub, formerly known as Molloy´s wine bar, was located in Falls road, West Belfast. The Laurel Leaf at this stage was frequented by political hardliners and nobody was really surprised, when the bar was bombed out on Paddy´s day 1973.
